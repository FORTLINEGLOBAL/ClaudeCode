## Brief 1: UAE Tier 1 Hospitality — GM + Security Director

**active:** true
**target_volume_per_week:** 8

- Focus on properties opened or expanded 2018–2025

- Standalone properties welcome if Tier 1 branding

### Target contacts (in priority order)
1. General Manager
2. Director of Security / Head of Security
3. VP Operations (if no direct security role exists)

### Geographic focus
- Dubai (DIFC, Downtown, JBR, Palm)
- Abu Dhabi (Corniche, Yas Island, Saadiyat)
- Sharjah, Ras Al Khaimah (emerging luxury)

### ICP fit signals (higher = better match)
- Property opened or significantly expanded in last 24 months
- Group-level expansion press releases
- Conference speaking by the GM or security director
- New leadership hire (GM or Security Director appointed in last 12 months)
- Published interview in trade press (HotelNewsMe, Arabian Business, Hotelier ME)

### Exclusions
- Budget/economy properties
- Serviced apartments
- Already contacted in last 120 days

Do not reference Israeli technology, Israeli partners, or any Israel-adjacent context for this brief.
